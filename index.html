Description

An easy chicken curry for an after school treat

Ingredients

  • 300g of chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 Jar of Madras curry paste
  • 2 red onions sliced
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin of coconut milk

Steps

  1. Add the sliced onions to a pan with 1 tbsp of olive oil
  2. Simmer until onions are soft
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and cook for 5 mins
  4. Add the copped tomatoes and bring to the boil
  5. reduce heat and simmer for 15 mins
  6. add the coconut milk and coo for a further five minutes
  7. server with basmati rice
