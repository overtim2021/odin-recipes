Lasagna

Description

Lasanga is a popluar baked pasta dish that is great for a week night meal!

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • White sauce
  • pasta sheets
  • Beef Stock
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Brown the mince in the a saucepan, no oil
  2. add Tomatoes and Beef stock
  3. simmer for 10 to 15 mins
  4. make the white sauce
  5. Add sprinkle of oregano to the beef/tomato mixture
  6. lay the pasta sheet in a non stick baking pan
  7. add a layer of beef mix
  8. add a layer of white sauce
  9. repeat as necessary
  10. top with mozarella
  11. bake in a oven at 200f for 40 mins
